WAITING LIST TABLE:

This Waiting list table shgows the customers waiting yet to be approached by any store attendee. This priority table is updated by the unattended time of the customers. The user managing the dashboard can send the emergency "attend" alert regarding that particular customer to all employees so they can attend that indivisual customer in best possible way.

TIP #1 :

The user managing the dashboard can click the button and confirmation message will pop up, after clicking button the message will be sent to the attendees.

Section Preferences of Customers

CUSTOMER PREFERENCE CHART:

This card visualizes the plotted polar chart of the the indivisual section preferences of the customers visiting the store. This makes it simple for the staff to identify the sections having maximum customer concentration so that enough staff can be present there to attend them thus improving management of the store.

TIP #1 :

Hover your mouse cursor over the chart to see and compare the datasets for each section prefered by customers.

TIP #2 :

Click indivisual index keys in the chart to disable the datasets of a specific section so as to focus on just one section.
