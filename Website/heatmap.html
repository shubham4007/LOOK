#Heatmaps

#NOTE :
This page is made to give the user an idea of how this webpage will be implemented after it is connected to the CCTV of the store. The current prototype is made using available sample videos and layout.

Considering the present situation that we are in, it is not suggessted that more than 3-4 people gather at one place. We are also aware that lockdown is open, so everybody is allowed to visit stores, shopping malls, supermarkets and retail stores without much knowledge about how safe the environment is. So to solve this problem we bring forward to you "LOOK"

Lets LOOK as somethings!

Consider a fashion store, LOOK counts the number of people present in every indivisual section of store like men, women, kids etc using the video recording accumalated using CCTV present in the store itself. In the sample video provided below you can see how LOOK counts the number of people entering and leaving the counter (right side mens and left side womens) and the head count is being taken.

Men's Section Footage

Working Details:
Up - Shows the number of people entering the men's section
Down - Shows the number of people leaving the men's section.

Womens Section Footage

Working Details:
Up - Shows the number of people entering the women's section
Down - Shows the number of people leaving the women's section.

This data is stored in the database and helps the stores in many ways. LOOK helps the managing staff of that store to maintain charts regarding the increment or decrement in customer count.

#QUESTION :
What help can LOOK provide during pandemic!?
Most productive one - HEATMAPS!!

Concentration of people in a particular section :
Red - Maximum
Yellow - Manageable
Green - Minimum

Heatmaps mainly show the concentration of people in one place which can lead to loss of safety during this time. So this chart can help the stores to make their management better and easier. The store management can be informed about the crowding and proper actions can be taken by the present staff to guide people to maintain proper and safer distance from each other. Hence, the outside social distancing can be improved leading to less spread of virus in malls, supermarkets etc.

# BIG PICTURE:
As you can see, the video sample above made using deep learning provides us with many features which acts as service for stores and malls to maintain a safe environment and to guide people through this crucial time.
Our aim is to not only become helpful for the fashion storeline but also for all available retail stores.
So let us all LOOK around and enjoy the time of our life without much worries
